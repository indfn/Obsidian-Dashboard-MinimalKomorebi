# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of the **Obsidian Dashboard Gallery** pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, or identity.

We are committed to maintaining an open, welcoming, and respectful space where creativity can thrive.

---

## Our Standards

**Behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Being respectful and kind in all interactions
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Crediting others' work when building upon it
- Focusing on what benefits the community

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Claiming others' work as your own without credit
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies across all community spaces — including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any space where you represent this project.

---

## Enforcement

The project maintainer is responsible for enforcing these standards. Violations may result in removal of contributions or a temporary/permanent ban from the community, depending on severity.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue or reaching out to the maintainer directly via GitHub. All reports will be handled with discretion.
